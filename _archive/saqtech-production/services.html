Our Services

From residential glass installations to industrial metal fabrication, we deliver excellence in every detail.

Glass & Glazing

Specialized in frameless glass partitions, shower enclosures, mirrors, and glass balustrades. We use tempered and laminated glass for maximum safety and style.

Pergolas & Gazebos

Custom-designed aluminum and wood-finish pergolas. Modern outdoor living solutions for Dubai villas.

Car Parking Shades

Heavy-duty cantilever and tensile fabric shades (HDPE/PVC) protecting vehicles from the harsh UAE sun.

CNC Cutting

Precision laser cutting for decorative metal panels, gates, and mashrabiya patterns. Gold and black finishes available.

Supply and installation of recess covers, drainage gratings, and industrial access covers.
