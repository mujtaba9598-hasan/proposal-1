Pergola

Transform outdoor spaces with our custom-designed pergolas. Combining style and functionality, our pergolas create shaded comfortable areas for relaxation, entertainment, or dining while enhancing the beauty of gardens, patios or rooftops.

CNC Panel

Discover precision-engineered CNC panels crafted with intricate designs to meet your architectural and decorative requirements. Our panels are ideal for adding a modern touch to interiors, exteriors and commercial spaces, ensuring unmatched quality and elegance.

Manhole & Gratings

We offer high-quality manhole covers and gratings designed for safety, durability, and performance. Perfect for industrial, commercial, and urban applications, our solutions withstand heavy loads while ensuring smooth functionality and compliance with industry standards.

Stainless Steel Work

Elevate your spaces with our expert stainless steel fabrication services. From custom designs to robust industrial components, our stainless steel solutions combine strength, durability, and elegance, catering to a wide rangeof residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

Handrails

Ensure safety and sophistication with our meticulously crafted handrails. Designed for durability and aesthetic appeal, our handrails are perfect for homes, offices and public spaces offering seamless integration with your architectural style.

Boundary Wall Fence

Enhance your property's safety and privacy with our durable boundary wall fencing solutions. Designed to be secure, long-lasting, and visually appealing, our fences are tailored to suit residential, commercial, and industrial needs blending functionality with aesthetics.

Car Parking Shades

Protect vehicles from harsh weather with our durable and stylish car parking shades. Designed for maximum coverage and resilience, our shades are ideal for residential, commercial and public parking areas, blending utility with aesthetics.

Our Services

Glass Work Solutions

Elevate your spaces with the elegance and versatility of glass. We specialize in custom glass solutions that combine functionality, style, and precision.

Glass Partitions

Transform your interiors with sleek and modern glass partitions. Ideal for offices, homes, and commercial spaces, our partitions create a sense of openness while maintaining privacy. With options ranging from frosted and tinted glass to frameless designs, we offer solutions that suit your aesthetic and functional requirements. Durable and easy to maintain, our glass partitions are designed to maximize natural light and enhance the visual appeal of your space.

Glass Pool Fence

Ensure safety without compromising style with our glass pool fences. Crafted from toughened, high-grade glass, our fences provide a secure barrier around pools while maintaining an unobstructed view. The minimalist and transparent design adds a touch of elegance to your outdoor area. Our glass pool fences comply with safety standards, ensuring peace of mind while enhancing the beauty of your property.

Mirror Work

Add sophistication and depth to your interiors with our custom mirror work. From decorative wall mirrors to functional bathroom and gym mirrors, we craft designs that reflect your style and meet your needs. Our mirrors are made from high-quality materials and come in a range of shapes, sizes, and finishes, making them perfect for enhancing light, creating the illusion of space, or serving as striking focal points.

Glazing Work

Our glazing services offer innovative solutions for windows, doors, facades, and more. Whether you need double-glazing for energy efficiency or specialized glazing for acoustic control, we deliver expert craftsmanship and premium materials. With a focus on performance and aesthetics, our glazing work is perfect for modern architectural projects, adding durability, functionality, and a refined look to your spaces.
